MODEL COURSE

Ancient Dinosaur Trails &
Valley Cycling Road

Magnificent valley views and
an adventure tracing 100-million-year-old memories.

An exhilarating course running through the "Kawashiro Valley" cycling road along the Sasayama River. It is a dedicated road for bicycles with no cars, so beginners and children can enjoy it safely.

Enjoy seasonal beauty: cherry blossoms in spring, fresh green in summer, and autumn leaves. The destination is the site where 100-million-year-old dinosaur footprint fossils were discovered. Let your imagination roam to ancient times.

Kawashiro Valley
Beautiful valley of strange rocks and clear streams accessed by bike.

Forest Bathing
Refresh by pedaling through the quiet cedar forest.

Dinosaur Discovery Site
Visit the actual geological layer where fossils were found.
