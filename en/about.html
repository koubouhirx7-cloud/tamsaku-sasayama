About TAMSAKU

PHILOSOPHY

Into the Unknown Time of Tamba

In an age where information is readily available, we seem to have fewer opportunities to think about the essence of things.
We believe that true travel memories are created not just by information from screens, but by actually experiencing and feeling the place.

Your guide, TAMSAKU, who knows everything about the Satoyama of Tamba-Sasayama, Hyogo, proposes experiences that can only be found here.
Why not set out on a journey to find a Tamba-Sasayama that no one else knows?

Of course, small children are welcome to participate.
