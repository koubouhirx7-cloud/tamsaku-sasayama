MODEL COURSE

Sasayamaguchi Station Start:
Relaxing Satoyama & Castle Town

A journey starting from the station.
Explore Tamba-Sasayama empty-handed.

This is a convenient plan where you can meet and finish at JR Sasayamaguchi Station, the gateway to Tamba-Sasayama. About 1 hour from JR Osaka Station. Your journey begins as soon as you get off the train.

The road from the station to the castle town transforms into an enjoyable cycling course. Head towards the historic castle town while viewing seasonal rural landscapes.

Start near Station
Easy access without a car, meeting directly at JR Sasayamaguchi Station.

Road to Castle Town
Peaceful Satoyama landscapes spread out on the way to the town.

Effortless E-BIKE
Even with distance, electric assist bicycles make moving comfortable.

ITINERARY

10:30

Meet at JR Sasayamaguchi Station

Meet the guide in front of the station. Bike adjustment and lecture.

11:00

Start Cycling

Depart towards the castle town. Ride through peaceful rural scenery.

12:00

Castle Town Lunch

Enjoy lunch using local ingredients (We can recommend places).

15:30

Arrive at JR Sasayamaguchi Station

Return to the station and disband. Buy souvenirs and head home by train.
