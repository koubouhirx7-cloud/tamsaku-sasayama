The Satoyama Experience

"Tamsaku" offers bicycle tours in Tamba-Sasayama, Kyoto's charming neighbor.
Here lies the "Satoyama" — Japan's traditional agricultural landscape where people and nature coexist in harmony.

Private Tours Only

We strictly limit our tours to one private group at a time. Enjoy the serene landscape at your own pace without any distractions.

Tailor-Made Journey

Whether you want to visit a specific pottery kiln, enjoy a cafe in a renovated folk house, or simply ride through the rice fields, we customize the itinerary to suit your interests.
