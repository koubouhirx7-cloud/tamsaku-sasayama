MODEL COURSE

Sakura Corridor Cycling:
Connecting with Nature & People

Into the breathtaking tunnel of cherry blossoms
coloring the spring of Tamba-Sasayama.

The "Sakura Corridor" stretches for approximately 4km along the Sasayama River. With about 1,000 Somei-Yoshino cherry trees in full bloom, this is one of the most famous cherry blossom spots in the region.

On this tour, we will ride E-BIKEs through the cherry blossom tunnel. Scenery that would pass by in an instant by car can be savored slowly on a bicycle. Enjoy a private time avoiding the crowds.

Sakura Tunnel
Cycling under the endless rows of cherry trees.

Spring in Satoyama
Enjoy not just cherry blossoms, but also rape blossoms and fresh greenery of the countryside.

Private Guide
We guide you to the best photo spots at your own pace.
